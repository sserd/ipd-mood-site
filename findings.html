Multi Tethers

Nowdays Single tether system has been used to deorbit the debris and we propose a new system which has multi tethers to do the same operation in an efficient way. We try to achieve the same potential of a single tether with multi tether system. Different parameters such as force,length,current are compared and the results obtained are similar. Hence of using multi tether system we can reduce the breakage of tethers and the goal of deorbiting can be done successfuly.

Distance between adjacent tethers :

Plasma influence on multiple conductors :

Consider a condition where two conductors are placed in the plasma, the positive ion sheath is formed as per the previous explanations in this paper. When these two conductors are placed very close to each other the net positive ions around each conductor reduces as there will be interference the sheath of adjacent conductors, resulting in the distribution of positive charges between the interfering sheaths. The reduction of positive ions in the sheath will in return reduce the number of electrons on the sheath-contact surface of the conductor resulting in the reduction of the current in the individual conductor. As the above condition also occurs when we place multiple tethers there is a need to find the minimal spacing between the adjacent tethers so that the reduction of induced currents in the individual tethers does not happen. So, we concluded that if each of the tethers is placed outside the sheath of its adjacent tether, the reduction of current will not happen. It was found that a minimum spacing of twice the sheath radius of the individual tether will prevent the above-mentioned issue, as there will be no interference between the sheath of adjacent tethers.

Deorbiting time due to changing current:

Multiple tether as not affecting the current in any other tether(this holds when sheath to sheath interactions do not affect the current in the tether)does not affect the de-orbiting time.
